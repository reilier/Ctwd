Mechanical Design and Structure

    
   

What is Mechanical Design?

       

Mechanical design focuses on the creation of structures and systems to ensure the mechanical functionality and stability of a robot or machine.

 
 
   

Key Components

       

Mechanical structures include frames, joints, actuators, and linkages that provide the necessary physical support and movement capabilities.

 
 
   

Tools and Techniques

       

Computer-aided design (CAD) and 3D modeling are commonly used to plan and simulate mechanical designs before manufacturing.
