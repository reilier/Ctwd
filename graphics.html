Graphic Designing Course

Course Introduction

Welcome to the Graphic Designing course! In this beginner-level course, you will learn the basics of graphic design and how to use design software to create simple but effective graphics.

What You'll Learn

  • Introduction to design software (Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, etc.)
  • Introduction to design software (Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, etc.)
  • Creating simple shapes and text effects
  • Working with color schemes
  • Basic image editing techniques
  • Designing simple logos and posters

Course Duration

This course is designed to be completed in 4 weeks. Each week you will complete practical exercises and gain hands-on experience with designing visuals.

Conclusion

By the end of this course, you will have a solid understanding of basic graphic design concepts and the confidence to start creating your own designs.
