Fundamentals of Electronics

    
   

What is Electronics?

       

Electronics is the branch of science and technology that deals with circuits, components, and systems involving the flow of electrons in conductors, semiconductors, and vacuum tubes.

 
 
   

Basic Components

       

Electronic components like resistors, capacitors, transistors, and diodes form the building blocks of circuits. Each component serves a specific function in the system.

 
 
   

Applications of Electronics

       

Electronics powers various devices, from smartphones and computers to medical equipment and industrial systems, revolutionizing modern life.
