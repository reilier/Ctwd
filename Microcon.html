Microcontrollers and Programming

    
   

What are Microcontrollers?

       

Microcontrollers are compact integrated circuits that act as mini-computers to control devices. They include a processor, memory, and input/output peripherals.

 
 
   

Programming Microcontrollers

       

Microcontrollers are programmed using languages like C, C++, or Python. Arduino and Raspberry Pi are popular platforms for robotics programming.
