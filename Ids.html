Introduction to Design Software

    
   

What is Design Software?

       

Design software helps creators bring their ideas to life digitally. Popular tools include Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, CorelDRAW, and free alternatives like GIMP and Inkscape.

 
 
   

Common Features

       

Features like layer management, vector editing, filters, and effects make it easy to create, edit, and enhance visuals efficiently.
